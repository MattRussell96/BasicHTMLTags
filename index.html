The Game My Mind Has Been On Lately

Since I couldn't think of something to make this page about, I figured I would make it about something that has my interest peaked currently!... Besides this class obviously.

This game is pretty awesome and unlike anything I have played in a long time. You could say for me it is breathing a breath of fresh air into a game genre that I thoroughly enjoy.

Really what I want to do is expose whom is reading this page to the masterpiece of an early release game this is. Below I will take you through a few things subjectively captivating about this game.

List on why this makes the list!


Every New Game Goal

  1. Get your briefing
  2. Pick up key to area
  3. Gather ghost hunting tools
  4. Familiarize yourself with the floor plans
  5. Collect/Compose yourself
  6. Enter area and begin hunt
  7. Find out where the ghost is and what type it is
  8. Collect evidence
  9. Get gone like the wind
  10. DONT DIE!
